# Simple Sentence

## Core

- 谓语动词分类
    - 实义动词: swim, dance. 根据这个动作是否可以由主语独立完成，分为:
        1. 及物动词 vt.:
        2. 不及物动词 vi.: 主语能自己独立完成这个动词
    - 非实义动词: 谓语动词没有实在的动作含义。be, seem
- 谓语动词的时态、语态、情态和否定的变化，无论变成几个单词，永远看做一个整体，即作为一个谓语动词

### 核心构成

#### 主谓

- 主谓 == 主语 + 不及物动词(vi.)
- He **cries**.
- ...about 7 million people **arrived**...
- Waiting list **increase** all the time...

#### 主谓宾

- 主谓宾 == 主语 + 及物动词(vt.) + 宾语
- We **need** progressive policies...
- I **understand** this.
- This argument **has attracted** a lot of attention...
- ...economic co-ordination **should involve** all 27 members of the EU club...

#### 主谓双宾

- 主谓双宾 == 主语 + 及物动词(vt.) + 两个宾语（人 + 物）
- It **gave** me the validation and confidence...
- Last month, Howard Schmidt...**offered** the federal government a proposal...
- 主谓双宾结构的句子有两种写法，且表意相同
    - Last month, Howard Schmidt...**offered** the federal government a proposal...
        - == Last month, Howard Schmidt...**offered** a proposal **to** the federal government...
        - 注意在"物+人"的情况下，中间要加入介词，通常为to或for
            - for 强调动作目的
            - to 强调动作方向
    - I bought a present for you.
        - == I bought you a present.

#### 主谓宾补

- 主谓宾补 == 主语 + 及物动词(vt.) + 宾语 + 宾语补足语（简称宾补）
- Furthermore, these losses **make** us mature...
- 宾补用来补充说明宾语，在宾语和宾补的内容中间加上中文"**是**"可以构成完整的句意
    - mature 宾补 => us **是** mature
- Pete Schneider **considers** the climb tougher today.
- Each book opens up new avenues of knowledge.
    - new avenues of knowledge 是一个 **名词短语**，整体充当宾语

#### 主系表

- 主系表 == 主语 + 系动词 + 表语

| link v.                         | 场景                       | Example                                                                                                  |
|---------------------------------|--------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| be动词                            | 单独使用，翻译成是                | More education **is** the new principle.<br>Teenagers **are** paradoxical.                               |
| get, became, turn, go, grow     | 变得、成为                    | These purchases often **become** more valuable with time...<br>Phones **become** our security blanket... |
| look, sound, smell, taste, feel | 感官动词，看、听、闻、尝、感觉**起来**... | ...the man **looked** puzzled and hurt.<br>...the zone's future **looks** uncertain at best              |
| remain/keep+adj.                | 保持...                    | It **remains** the world's largest trading block.                                                        |
| seem+adj.                       | 似乎...                    | But the discussions now **seem** out of date.                                                            |
| prove                           | 证明，显示出                   | ...the retreat from predominance **proved** painful                                                      |

#### 主谓宾 vs 主谓宾补 vs 主谓双宾

1. The teacher teaches English. -> 主谓宾
2. The teacher teaches us English. -> 主谓双宾
3. The teacher makes us knowledgeable. -> 主谓宾补

### Words

- waiting list
    - 候补名单；等候审批的申请人名单
- all the time
    - 一直
- co-ordination
    - 协作
- federal
    - adj. 联邦的
- validation
    - n. 认可，肯定
- furthermore
    - adv. 此外；而且
- mature
    - adj. 成熟的
- outdated
    - adj. 过时的，陈旧的
- look puzzled
    - 看起来很困惑、茫然
- paradoxical
    - adj. 自行矛盾的，看似荒谬的
- at best
    - 充其量，至多
- trading block
    - 贸易区块；贸易集团
- out of date
    - 过时
