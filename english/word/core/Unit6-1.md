## tend

- vi. 往往会；趋向
- 词组
    - tend to do sth. 往往，总是
- tendency
    - n. 倾向；趋势
    - Involuntary part-time employment in the U.S. shows a general **tendency** of
      decline.
        - involuntary [ɪnˈvɑlənteri]
            - adj. 非自愿的
            - Similar: involution n. 内卷；卷绕，回旋; 复归; 包卷; 对合
- trend [trend]
    - n. 趋势
    - embrace this irreversible trend 拥抱（接受）这一不可逆转的趋势
- inclination [ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃn]
    - n. 倾向
    - == tendency n. 倾向；趋势
    - inclination for risk-taking 冒险倾向
    - incline [ˈɪnklaɪn]
        - vt.（使）倾斜; （使）偏向，倾向于
        - vi. （使）倾斜; （使）偏向，倾向于
    - inclined
        - adj. 想（做某事）的；有...倾向的
        - 词组
            - be inclined to do sth. 往往做某事/倾向做某事
                - == tend to do sth.
                - == be prone to do sth.
- prone [proʊn]
    - adj. 有做（坏事）的倾向的；(-prone)有...倾向的
    - == inclined adj. 想（做某事）的；有...倾向的
    - be prone to noun/doing sth. 有...的倾向/容易...
    - Another nationwide concern is whether public funds from other agencies are going into construction in
      **fire-prone** districts.
- tide [taɪd]
    - n. 潮；潮流
    - Similar: tiny [ˈtaɪni] adj. 极小的，微小的
    - Time and tide wait for no man.

- refrain [rɪˈfreɪn]
    - vi. 避免；克制；制止
    - n. 经常重复的评价（或抱怨）；老话，陈词滥调（cliche [klɪˈʃeɪ]）

    - 词组
        - refrain **from** noun/doing sth. 克制/避免做某事

    - When someone burps in a quiet classroom it can be hard to **refrain from laughing**.
    - Complaints about poor food in schools have become a *familiar([fəˈmɪliər] adj. 熟悉的)* **refrain**.
    - The decline in American manufacturing is a common **refrain**, particularly from Donald Trump.
