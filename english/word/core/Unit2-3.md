## test

- testify
    - v. 证实；作证
    - == prove == verify == confirm
    - Anyone who has toiled through SAT will **testify** that test-taking skill also matters, whether it's knowing when
      to guess or what questions to skip.
- contest
    - vt. 争辩；争取赢得（比赛、选举等）
    - n. 比赛；争夺
    - In Arizona vs. United States, the majority *overturned* three of the four **contested** provisions of Arizona's
      controversial plan.
        - provision
            - n. 规定，条款；供应品
            - == rule
        - controversial [ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃl] adj. 引起争论的 😔

- protest
    - vt. 抗议
    - n. 抗议
    - -pre 在...前
        - == before
    - -pro 向前
        - == forward
    - **Protests** have erupted over construction of the Thirty Meter Telescope(TMT), a giant observatory that promises
      to revolutionize humanity's view of the cosmos.
- verify
    - vt. 证实；核实
    - When you **verify** something, you show that **it's true**.
- prove
    - vt. 证实
    - v. 证明是
    - == verify
    - proof
        - n. 证据
        - adj. 防...的
        - water-proof

## different

- adj. 不同的
- differ
    - vi. 不同与；持不同看法
    - A differ **from** B
    - Ways of reading on the train or in bed are likely to **differ** considerably **from** reading in a seminar room.
- difference n. 差异；差额
- differentiate [ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt]
    - v. 区分
    - vt. 使有差别
    - == distinguish == separate [ˈsepəreɪt]
- distinguish
    - v. 辨别
    - vt. 区分；使具有...特色
    - == separate
    - distinguish A from B
    - Quinn and her colleagues conducted a test to see if rats can **distinguish** a friendly rat **from** a hostile
      one.
    - Contrast: distinguish vs. differ
        - Could he <ins>distinguish/differ</ins> right from wrong?
        - The two leaders had <ins>distinguish/differ</ins> on the issue.
- distinction
    - n. 差别
    - distinctive
        - adj. 独特的
        - == special
        - Nothing stops you from being **distinctive**.
        - distinctiveness n. 特有性
    - distinctly adv. 明显地
