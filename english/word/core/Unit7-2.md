    - 动词 + 介词 + 宾语
    - He **sleeps** early.

## sense

- vt. 检测出；感觉到
- n. 感觉，意识；判断力；意义
- Similar: scene n. 场面；情景；现象
- SENSE AND SENSIBILITY
- 词组
    - make sense 有意义；说得通；有道理
    - in one sense 在某种意义上
    - common sense 常识
    - a sense of + noun 一种...感；...意识；...感觉
- nonsense n. 胡扯 😔
- sensible
    - adj. 明智的，理性的
    - **Sensible** ideas have been around for a long time, but the state-level bodies that govern the profession have
      been too conservative to implement them.
        - profession n. 职业；专业
- sensitive
    - adj. 体贴的；须谨慎对待的；（对低温、光、食物等）过敏的；（对细微变化）灵敏的
    - 词组
        - be sensitive to 对...敏感
    - Until this *century([ˈsentʃəri])*, university libraries were not very price **sensitive**.
    - sensitivity n. （信息、话题等的）敏感性，体贴
    - insensitive adj. 不敏感的
- sensibility n. 情感; 敏感性，灵敏度; 感觉，感性; 感光性，感光度
- sentiment [ˈsentɪmənt]
    - n. 观点；情绪（多愁善感）
    - At the next election none of the big parties seem likely to endorse this **sentiment**.
        - endorse [ɪnˈdɔːrs]
            - v. 赞同，支持
            - Similar: endure
                - [ɪnˈdʊr]
                - vt. 忍受
                - vi. 持续
- emotion
    - n. 情绪
    - emotional adj. 情感上的；情绪的

## effort

- n. 努力；有组织的活动
- 词组
    - make an effort to do sth. 努力做某事
        - == make efforts to do sth.
        - == endeavor to do
        - == strive to do
    - take efforts 付出努力
- We should **spare no efforts to** protect the environment.
    - spare [sper]
        - vt. 抽出时间，节约，吝啬
        - adj. 空闲的，备用的，多余的
        - n. 备用品
    - spare no efforts to 不遗余力
        - == make great efforts to
- endeavor [ɪnˈdɛvər]
    - vi. 努力
    - Similar: endure [ɪnˈdʊr]
        - vt. 忍受
        - vi. 持续
    - 词组
        - endeavor to do
            - == make efforts to
    - ...endeavor to live a joyful life
    - California's Forest Carbon Plan **endeavors to reduce** the *density([ˈdensəti] n. 密度)* of some of its forests.
- strive [straɪv]
    - vi. 努力
    - 词组
        - strive to do
    - The most successful monarchies **strive to** abandon or hide their old aristocratic ways.
        - monarchy [ˈmɑːnərki] n. 君主政体
        - aristocratic [əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪk] adj. 贵族的
