## attract

- [əˈtrækt]
- v. 吸引；引起（反应）（at(强调)-tract(拉；拽)）
- -tract- 拉；拽
- 词组
    - be attracted to sth. 被某物吸引
- attraction n. 吸引；向往的地方；有吸引力的事
- attractive
    - adj. 吸引人的
    - attractively adv. 有吸引力地
    - unattractive adj. 没有吸引力的
- attractiveness n. 吸引力
- extract [ɪkˈstrækt]
    - vt. （用力）拔出
    - ex- 外
        - == exit
    - So he hired a dentist to transplant nine teeth into his jaw—having **extracted** them from the mouths of his
      slaves.
        - dentist [ˈdentɪst] n. 牙科医生
        - transplant vt. 使迁移；移植（器官、皮肤、头发等）
- contract [ˈkɑːntrækt]
    - v. （使）收缩
    - n. 合同
    - contrast
        - vt. 对比
        - n. 差别
- abstract [ˈæbstrækt]
    - adj. 抽象派的；抽象的
    - n. 摘要
    - abstract writing 摘要写作
    - There's an **abstract** or summary at the top of first page of the article you copied.

## charge

- vt. 控告；指责；使...承担责任
- v. （向...）收费；给...充电
- n. 指控；主管
- 词组
    - in charge (of) 负责
- charged
    - adj. 充满情绪的、带有某种强烈色彩的
    - politically charged 政治意味浓厚的；有政治争议的
    - emotionally charged 情绪化的；充满情感的
- accuse [əˈkjuːz]
    - vt. 指责；指控
    - Still, we need to be careful not to **accuse** opponents **of** bad arguments too quickly.
        - accuse sb. of sth. 指责某人某事
            - == charge sb. with sth.
        - opponent [əˈpoʊnənt] n. 对手，竞争者；反对者
- expense [ɪkˈspens]
    - n. 费用（一般 小）
    - There seems to be a predominance of short-term thinking at the **expense** of long-term investing.
        - predominance [prɪˈdɑmɪnəns]
            - n. （一类人或物在数量上的）优势
            - == dominance n. 统治地位
        - a predominance of ...占主导地位/占多数/占优势
        - at the expense of 在牺牲（或损害）...的情况下
        - there seems to be 似乎存在
    - expenditure [ɪkˈspendɪtʃər] n. 开支（官方 大）

## special

- adj. 特殊的；有专门目的的
- This law abolished **special** educational requirements for federal jurors.
    - abolish [əˈbɑːlɪʃ] vt. 废除（法律、制度、习俗等）
- specially adv. 专门地
- specialized adj. 专门的；专业的
- especial [ɪˈspeʃl]
    - adj. 特别的，突出的
    - especially [ɪˈspeʃəli]
        - adv. 尤其
        - == particularly adv. 特别；尤其
- particular
    - adj. 特指的；特殊的；尤其的
    - 词组
        - in particular 尤其是、特别是
    - Even better would be to help elevate notions of beauty beyond the material standards of a **particular** industry.
        - even better would be to... 更好的做法/选择是... => 倒装句
        - help do sth. 帮助做某事
            - == help sb. do sth.
    - In a move that has intellectual-property lawyers abuzz, the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit said it
      would use a **particular** case to conduct a broad review of business-method patents.
    - particularly
        - adv. 特别；尤其
        - == especially [ɪˈspeʃəli] adv. 尤其
- specific [spəˈsɪfɪk]
    - adj. 明确的；特定的
    - She declined to be more **specific** about the reasons for the separation.
        - decline
            - vi. 下降；衰退(PE)
            - v. 谢绝(PE)
            - n. 下降；衰退
            - == descend [dɪˈsend] v. 下来
    - specifically
        - adv. 明确地
        - I **specifically** told you not to go near the water!
- unique
    - adj. 独一无二的；独特的；独有的
    - uniqueness n. 独特性
