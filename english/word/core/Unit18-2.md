- 感情色彩正向: 🙂

- vt. = transitive verb（及物动词）
    - 动词 + 宾语
    - 后面必须接宾语
    - **不**需要加介词
    - He **likes** apples
- vi. = intransitive verb（不及物动词）
    - 动作**不需要对象**就能完整表达意思
    - 动词 + 介词 + 宾语
    - He **sleeps** early.
- [e] -> 哎(ai)
- [ə] -> 额(e)
- 破折号: —
- C: Cause
- R: Result
- Opposite: 反义词 
- Similar: 形近词 
- Contrast: 对比

## monarch

- [ˈmɑːnərk]
- n. 君主
- monarchy [ˈmɑːnərki] n. 君主政体
- reign [reɪn]
    - vi. 统治
    - n. 君主统治时期
    - Rain falls from above, and people who **reign** rule from a position above others.
        - rule from/rule over 统治某个地方/人民
    - He used to **reign** over popular music, always topping the charts.
    - "The ancient Hawaiians were *astronomers([əˈstrɑːnəmər] n. 天文学家)*", wrote Queen Liliuokalani, Hawaii's
      last <ins>**reigning** monarch(统治地位的君主)</ins>, in 1987.
- throne [θroʊn] n. 王座
- sovereign [ˈsɑːvrɪn]
    - adj. 有主权的
    - ...a painful reminder of the occupation of what was once a **sovereign** nation. ...这是对那个曾经主权独立的国家如今遭受占领的惨痛提醒
    - A move back to self-sufficiency, <ins>the argument goes(有说法认为)</ins>, would boost the farming industry,
      political **sovereignty** and even the nation's health.
